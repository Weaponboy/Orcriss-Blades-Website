Perfectly you!

When it comes to your knife design, there are a number of elements that you can customise to make your blade unique and perfectly suited to your needs and preferences. We will happily work with you to design and create the perfect knife. There are a number of elements for you to consider below, and each will potentially impact the final price of the knife.

Blade length and shape

Although there are standard blade shapes, we can adapt and tweak any design to fit your needs and preferences.

Tang and pin style

There are two different styles of knife tangs, one is called hidden tang and is as the name suggests the tang is hidden in the handle. The other is full tang where you can see the tang all the way around the handle.

Handle material and shape

We can source any wood you might want for your handle but have some standard woods that we will have in stock. We can make your handle to any shape you would like, here are some handle shape ideas

Handle inlays

We can do custom spine work with a design of your choice. We can also do custom inlay using copper, brass or stainless steel.

Blade finishes 

We can do different blade finishes this includes polished, black and a two tone finish.

Leather Sheaths

We can make you a custom hand stiched leather sheath that will allow you carry your knife with you anywhere you want to take it

The Orcriss Team

We are Josh and Ben, brothers who make the knives happen! We live on a farm just outside of Robertson, South Africa. We first became interested in knife making a few years ago when with our friends we organized a "weapons day" where we all competed in a weapons making competition. That is when we made our first blade and from there we kept on improving our knife making game.
